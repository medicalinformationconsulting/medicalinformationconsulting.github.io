Services

What We Offer

Parsing medical terminology, knowing the standards of practice, and identifying unacceptible medical error is not only challenging, but also consumes time and resources that can be better spent on what you do best. When you consult with us, each case is reviewed by an MD who has the knowledge to address these medical aspects of the case. We summarize your case's lengthy and complex medical information in a concise manner that can be easily understood by a non-medical audience.

Our services are customized to the particular needs of the case and address the pertinent legal questions you identify. You will receive a summary report of our overall impression and findings. In general, our medical case review includes one or more of the following services:

  • Screening cases for merit
  • Identifing deviations from standards of practice
  • Organizing and summarizing medical records
  • Researching and reporting on medical facts
  • Reviewing current medical literature

Who You Are

You are a plaintiff, defense counsel, insurance company, or individual who does not wish to commit your resources to interpreting complex medical records and instead wants to devote your time to your area of expertise. We work directly with you to ensure that your goals and questions are addressed in a cost-efficient manner.

Pricing

Our goal is to have a straightforward and transparent pricing model. We bill at an hourly rate of $200 at 15 minute increments for our review services. The initial consultation, wherein we discuss the case to be reviewed, as well as uncomplicated coordination and management of the case, are not billed. We will provide an estimated cost of service during the initial consultation.

About

Mission

Our goal is to provide concise, comprehensive, and cost-effective medical-legal review services to a discerning set of clients. When they use our services, they will have saved both time and money.

Who We Are

Medical Information Consulting, LLC is based in Nashville, Tennessee and serves clients throughout the state. It was founded in 2019 by Dr. Ryan Brancamp, who recognized that while the fields of medicine and law frequently overlap, there exists a knowledge gap between the two. We aim to bridge this gap through our consultative services with our legal colleagues.

Ryan Brancamp, MD, MPA

Ryan Brancamp is the founder and chief consultant of Medical Information Consulting, LLC. He is a graduate of Northwestern University Feinberg School of Medicine (MD, 2018) and of Indiana University School of Public and Environmental Affairs (MPA, 2009).
